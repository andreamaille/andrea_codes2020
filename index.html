About Me.

Hello! My name is Andrea Maille and I am a front end developer based in Toronto, Canada. I graduated from Juno College’s Immersive Web Development Program in 2019 and currently work at Commerce Dynamics developing ecommerce and loyalty websites for Fortune 500 companies. My favourite thing about coding continues to be seeing art, music, movement, design and creative thinking all come together to create new spaces online.

When I’m not coding, you can catch me building a spotify playlist, playing cello, working on my Etsy store or bullet journalling.
